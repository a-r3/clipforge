# Presets

This document lists the available presets supported by the Auto Scenario Video Builder. Presets help configure platform‑specific or style‑specific parameters without specifying each option individually.

## Platform presets

- **reels**: 9:16 aspect ratio and duration suitable for Instagram Reels.
- **tiktok**: 9:16 aspect ratio and pacing tailored to TikTok.
- **youtube-shorts**: 9:16 aspect ratio and compliance with YouTube Shorts guidelines.
- **landscape**: 16:9 aspect ratio for traditional horizontal video.

## Style presets

- **clean**: Minimal text overlays and subtle animations.
- **bold**: Larger fonts and higher contrast visuals.
- **minimal**: Reduced text and simplified design.
